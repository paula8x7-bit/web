ABOUT ME

Clinical judgment for real lives

I'm Paula Rubio, a registered dietitian-nutritionist with a clinical and integrative approach. I work with personalized diet therapy for body composition, digestive health, autoimmunity, hormonal imbalances, fatigue and other cases that call for looking beyond an isolated meal plan.

I see the body as a connected system. Digestion, energy, rest, lab results, symptoms, stress, training and life context are all part of the same story. That's why I don't work with rigid diets or one-size-fits-all protocols.

After 19 years working long-haul flights, I know what it means to try to take care of yourself between shifts, jet lag, travel and ever-changing routines. I don't design strategies for perfect weeks — I design them for real life.
